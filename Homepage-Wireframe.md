## Section Intent (for Phase 4 Homepage Creative Review)

| # | Section | Job to be done | Conversion path |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 1 | Navigation | Orient + persistent WhatsApp access | WhatsApp CTA |
| 2 | Cinematic Hero | Transformation promise + instant trust + immediate contact | Get Quote / WhatsApp / Text |
| 3 | Trust Bar | Reassure (insured, free quotes, workmanship, res+commercial) | — |
| 4 | Services Overview | Outcome-led summary of 4 services | Tap → service detail |
| 5 | Before / After Showcase | Prove transformation quality | Request similar transformation |
| 6 | Featured Projects | Depth of portfolio storytelling | View transformation |
| 7 | Why Choose Us | Differentiation (6 premium cards) | — |
| 8 | Service Areas | Local relevance + SEO | Tap → location page |
| 9 | Testimonials | Social proof | — |
| 10 | Contact | Final, frictionless conversion | WhatsApp / SMS / Call |
| 11 | Footer | Navigation, NAP, legal, schema | WhatsApp / Call |

**Persistent overlay (all viewports):** floating WhatsApp + SMS + Call actions, always visible.
