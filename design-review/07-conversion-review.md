# Deliverable 7 — Conversion Review (Phase 6)

**Test applied to every section:** *"Does this move the user closer to contacting the business?"* If not → redesign.

## Conversion hierarchy (mandated)
1. **WhatsApp** (primary)
2. **SMS** (secondary)
3. **Phone call** (third)
4. Contact form (optional, never the primary path)

## Persistent floating contact stack
- Bottom-right, thumb-reachable, on **every page and every scroll depth**.
- WhatsApp / SMS / Call, always visible.
- WhatsApp opens with a **pre-filled message**: *"Hi Propertycare, I'd like a quote for a transformation."*
- No competitor offers this — instant, uncontested conversion advantage.

## One dominant verb, repeated
Calm, consultative, premium-matched: **"Book a free survey"** / **"Tell us about your space"** repeated at hero, after the showcase, after services, and in the final CTA band — each with friction-killers: *"2 mins · no obligation · free, no-pressure quote."*

## Section-by-section conversion path

| Section | Moves toward contact? | Conversion device |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Hero | ✅ | Green "Book a free survey" + WhatsApp + Text |
| Trust bar | ✅ (removes risk) | Live rating chip; lowers anxiety before the ask |
| Services | ✅ | Each card → service page with its own contextual CTA |
| Before/After showcase | ✅✅ | "Request a similar transformation" |
| Transformations | ✅✅ | Case studies are the sales asset → CTA on each |
| Why Choose Us | ✅ (removes risk) | Reinforces trust → CTA band follows |
| Process module | ✅ | Demystifies engagement → "Start with a free survey" |
| Service areas | ✅ | Location page → local CTA |
| Testimonials | ✅ | Embedded reviews → CTA |
| B2B band | ✅ | Dedicated landlord/agent/developer enquiry route |
| FAQ | ✅ | Removes final objections → CTA |
| Contact | ✅✅ | WhatsApp/SMS/Call + short form w/ photo upload |
| Footer | ✅ | WhatsApp + Call + NAP |

## Service-specific conversion CTAs ([skill.md](../skill.md) rule)
- Painting → "Request a painting estimate"
- Electrical → "Speak with an electrician"
- Media Walls → "Get a media wall quote"
- Maintenance → "Arrange property maintenance"

## Form design (secondary path)
- Short, **progressively enhanced** (works without JS — directly fixing Foxglove's on-load error failure).
- Photo upload ("show us your space"), topic routing (homeowner / landlord / agent / developer), GDPR consent + spam protection.
- Friction-killer microcopy; confirmation state with WhatsApp fallback.

## Top-of-funnel capture
Lead magnet for the not-ready-yet: a media-wall planning guide / premium-finish lookbook (email capture) — wins prospects competitors lose entirely.

## Trust = conversion
Embedded live Google/Checkatrade widget (real stars + count) + AggregateRating schema; badge bar repeated top and bottom; explicit risk-reversal near every CTA (fixed itemised quotes, written guarantee, "we treat your home as our own — clean, tidy, respected").

## Anti-patterns explicitly avoided
No off-site redirects (NE Electrical/ServiceM8), no dead-end CTAs (Tyneside), no broken/demo forms (SAS), no conflicting/placeholder numbers (Paul Douglas), no popups. One consistent NAP + business landline for credibility.
