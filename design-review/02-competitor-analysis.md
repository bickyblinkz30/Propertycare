## Market-wide weaknesses (true across *every* area)

### SEO
- **Zero structured data anywhere.** Not one competitor across all 7 areas ships LocalBusiness / Service / Review / FAQ JSON-LD. **No rich results, no SERP star ratings, market-wide.** This is the single biggest uncontested win.
- Thin, single-page architectures (David Smith, J Brien, AB, KF, GMW) cap keyword coverage; few have town-level pages.
- Mis-targeted local signals: NE Electrical omits its own Washington base; IMC's *Durham* media-wall page uses *Stanley/Consett* alt text; titles lead with "Homepage" or omit location.
- Keyword-stuffed AI doorway pages (IMC, Media Wall Builders' "across the Seaham") that Google discounts.

### Design
- Dated WordPress / Duda / hibu / WordPress.com templates; tiny low-res logos; stock photography; 2013-era imagery; expiring CDN URLs.
- Thin galleries (Foxglove ~11 images) with no curated before/after sets.
- Template debris that destroys credibility: Paul Douglas shows `555-555-5555`; KF shows "Click on this text to edit it"; SAS leaks "roofing & guttering" copy; Tyneside renders literal "Button" labels; North East Electricals leaks raw menu JSON.

### UX
- Multiple H1s or zero H1s; near-empty rendered content (Paul Douglas ~811 words, 0 headings).
- Empty / placeholder alt text and `xxxx.jpg` filenames (Beam, Aerial World, IMC).
- Forms that degrade to visible errors on load (Foxglove).

### Conversion
- **No WhatsApp, no sticky mobile contact, no on-page quote form** in most.
- Off-site redirects (NE Electrical → ServiceM8), dead-end CTAs (Tyneside "FROM £1995" buttons loop to the same page), broken/demo forms (SAS "Contact Form Demo").
- Conflicting/placeholder phone numbers (Paul Douglas has 3 + a placeholder; KF lists two conflicting landlines); personal Hotmail/mobile-only contact (J Brien, David Smith).
- Unverifiable trust: plain-text "5.0 Google Rating" with no embedded widget; stale awards (NE Electrical 2015/16); unattributed testimonials; missing NICEIC/Part P/insurance/guarantee badges near CTAs.

### Category structure
- **Every competitor is single-trade or muddled** (painting-only, electrical-only, aerials-first retail, or bolt-on plumbing/boilers). Media-wall buyers are especially poorly served — Washington Fireplaces and Aerial World *outsource* installation; Beam and Media Wall Builders use boilerplate with placeholder imagery.

## What Propertycare can do better — the winning strategy

1. **Own the category.** The *only* premium single-team "whole-property transformation" brand in the North East: painting & decorating + electrical & lighting + bespoke media walls + property maintenance under one accountable, certified team. Sell the integrated package (a media wall = joinery + electrics + decorating in one quote) no single-trade rival can match.
2. **Make "Transformations" the hero.** Real before/after photo shoot, cinematic named/dated case studies, drag-wipe sliders — the hook the whole category sells on but *none* exploit.
3. **Dominate technical + local SEO uncontested.** LocalBusiness + Service + FAQPage + Review/AggregateRating + BreadcrumbList JSON-LD on every page, plus genuinely unique service × town landing pages with real local proof (Concord, Sulgrave, NE37/38, Seaham SR7, Low Fell, Whickham, Gosforth, Jesmond) — capturing star ratings no rival earns.
4. **Stack verifiable trust as a centrepiece.** Embedded live Google/Checkatrade widget with real star count; named, photo-attributed testimonials tied to specific transformations; badge bar (NICEIC/Part P, TrustMark, Dulux Select, fully insured, written workmanship guarantee) repeated top and bottom.
5. **Crush conversion friction.** Persistent floating WhatsApp (primary) → SMS → phone on every page; low-friction "Tell us about your space / Book a free survey" form with photo upload and friction-killer microcopy; one consistent NAP + business landline.
6. **Elevated language.** "Transformations" not "jobs"; a one-line design philosophy per service; calm consultative CTA paired with instant low-commitment contact.
7. **Authority content layer.** Local cost guides, "media wall with fire and electrics explained", EICR/landlord-safety guidance, dated "recent transformations" feed — topical authority the thin competitor sites cannot match.
