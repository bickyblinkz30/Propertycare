# Deliverable 6 — Homepage Creative Review (Phase 4)

Reviewing [Homepage-Wireframe.md](../Homepage-Wireframe.md) against the premium research and brand non-negotiables. Each section gets a verdict + the refinement applied.

## Hero — must answer the 3 questions
- **What transformation?** Headline states positioning: *"Transforming North East properties with precision, craft & care."* Ambient finished-room video **or** a single before/after wipe behind it.
- **Why trust them?** Brass eyebrow ("PREMIUM PROPERTY TRANSFORMATION · SUNDERLAND & THE NORTH EAST"); trust badges visible immediately below the fold (insured · guaranteed · certified).
- **Contact immediately?** Primary green **"Book a free survey"** + **"WhatsApp us"** + **"Text us"**, plus the persistent floating contact stack.
- **Verdict:** ✅ Strong. Refinement: keep H1 to one line on mobile; ensure the LCP image/video is optimised and the H1 is real text (not baked into the video).

## Trust Bar
- Insured · Free quotes · Professional workmanship · Residential & Commercial.
- **Refinement:** upgrade from plain text to a **recognisable badge row** (NICEIC/Part P, TrustMark, Dulux Select, Fully Insured, Written Guarantee) — verifiable trust is the centrepiece competitors lack. Add a live Google rating chip here.
- **Verdict:** ✅ with upgrade applied.
